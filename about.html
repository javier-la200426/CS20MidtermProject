About Us

Located in the Tsungming Tsu Complex (TTC), Kindlevan Café is a Tufts Dining location serving coffee, smoothies, pastries, and hot pressed sandwiches Monday through Friday.

Sustainability

  • Food Waste Composting - All waste is turned into compost
  • Partnership with Foley Fish - Responsibly harvested seafood from George's Bank, the Gulf of Maine, and the Bay of Fundy
  • Local Produce - Ingredients used are sourced from farms within 250 miles of Tufts University
  • Plant-Based Products - Products avoid the use of processed foods
  • Energy Efficiency - Use of solar power to operate equipment
